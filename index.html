Meet the Maker

I set up Ethereal Glass in 2018. My degree was from the National College of Art and Design in Dublin, where my specialisation was in Glass. This involved mostly glass blowing and fusing. After leaving college, due to my continued love of melting glass, I took up lampworking as a hobby. Lampworking is a type of glasswork where a propane torch is used to melt glass rods or tubes. Everything on this page was made by lampworking. After a few friends commissioned some beads, I decided to set up Ethereal Glass as a business.
